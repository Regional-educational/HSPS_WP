Classes We Offer

Pre KG

Primary education is the first stage of compulsory education. It enables the children to get prepared for future higher classes.

Age limit for admission in nursery is 2 years 6 months to 3 years 6 months.

Junior KG

Children are exposed to a lot of basic preschool learning activities that help them to get independent faster. These preschool activities help develop in children many self-help qualities like eating food themselves, dressing up, maintaining cleanliness and other such basic qualities.

Age limit for LKG (Junior KG) is 3 years 6 months to 4 years 6 months.

Senior KG

Age limit for for UKG (senior KG) is 4 years 6 months to 5 years 6 months.

Grade I to V

The primary stage consists of Classes I to V. i.e. of five years duration.

Age limit for class I is 5 yrs. 6 months to 6 yrs. 6 months, for class II is 6 yrs. 6 months to 7 yrs. 6 months and so on.

Why Choose Us

Best School in town

Parental Involvement

Many schools do not stress parental involvement; but we pull parents in and help them understand what they can do. The more we involve parents, the better students will behave and perform. Many parents want to know what's going on in class but have no way of figuring out how to do this.

Student-Centered

To be truly effective, HSPS's principal creates a system of core values that the entire staff shares. A common theme to each of the core values should be a student-centered view of education.

Mentoring Program

We provide new teachers with a mentor to help new teachers to understand the culture of the school and navigate its bureaucracy in areas as diverse as field trip procedures and purchasing classroom supplies.
